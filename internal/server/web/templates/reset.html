Fully uninstall Tallywell

Resetting removes your data but leaves the app installed. To remove Tallywell completely:

{{with .Content}} {{if eq .Platform "darwin"}}
  1. Click Delete all my data above (or delete {{.DataDir}} manually in Finder).
  2. Open your Applications folder, right-click Tallywell, and choose Move to Trash.
  3. Empty the Trash.
{{else if eq .Platform "windows"}}
  1. Click Delete all my data above (or delete {{.DataDir}} manually in Explorer).
  2. Open Settings → Apps, find Tallywell, and click Uninstall.
{{else}}
  1. Click Delete all my data above (or delete {{.DataDir}} manually).
  2. Delete the Tallywell binary or AppImage from wherever you placed it.
{{end}} {{end}}
