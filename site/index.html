Tallywell

One clear picture of your sessions and income.

For therapists juggling several payers — insurance platforms, private pay, and an employer. See what you've earned, what's still owed, and how your caseload looks, without logging into four portals.

Local-first. No servers, no accounts. Your client data never leaves your computer, and it's encrypted on disk.

Download

What it does

  • Log sessions by hand, or import a payer's CSV export.
  • Automatically reconciles sessions against payouts that arrive weeks later.
  • Shows income by source and month, and what's still outstanding.
  • Keeps employer (W-2) income separate from your own practice for taxes.
  • Exports a clean spreadsheet for you or your accountant anytime.

Installing on macOS

The app isn't notarized yet, so the first time you open it macOS will warn that it's from an unidentified developer. To allow it once: right-click the downloaded file in Finder, choose Open, then confirm. After that it opens normally and launches in your browser.

Open source

Tallywell is MIT-licensed and developed in the open, so anyone can verify it really is local-first. Contributions — especially CSV importers for more payers — are welcome.
